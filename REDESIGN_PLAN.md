# SOMOS.tech Website Redesign - Strategic Plan

## Organization Overview
- **Mission**: Empower Hispanic, Latinx, and allied professionals in tech
- **Model**: 100% volunteer-run 501(c)(3) nonprofit
- **Key Focus Areas**: Community, Programs, Mentorship, Career Development
- **Badge Programs**: Career Pioneer, AI Builder, Pathfinder, Guide
- **Geographic Reach**: 26 groups across 6+ chapters

## Design Philosophy
- **Modern & Clean**: Contemporary design with clear hierarchy
- **Community-First**: Emphasize connection and belonging
- **Programs-Centric**: Highlight opportunities and growth
- **Fluid & Connected**: Seamless navigation between community and programs
- **Inclusive**: Celebrate diversity with vibrant, welcoming aesthetic

## Core Pages & Navigation Structure

### Main Navigation
1. **Home** - Hero, mission, quick stats, programs showcase
2. **Community** - Groups, members, online community link, events
3. **Programs** - AI Career Roadmap, Mentorship, Labs, Career Resources
4. **Events** - Calendar view, filtering by location/type
5. **Groups** - Directory of local chapters with member count
6. **Get Involved** - Volunteer, Donate, Mentor
7. **Donate** - Support the mission

### Secondary Features
- Online Community (Discord/chat integration)
- Badge system showcase
- Program enrollment/tracking
- Member directory (with privacy controls)
- Blog/Resources

## Design System

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: #00FF91 (SOMOS green - vibrant, modern)
- **Secondary**: #051323 (Dark navy - trust, tech)
- **Accent**: #00D4FF (Cyan - innovation)
- **Backgrounds**: #0D1F2D to #0A1628 (Deep tech blue)
- **Text**: White (#FFFFFF), Light Gray (#E0E0E0)

### Typography
- **Headers**: Bold, modern sans-serif (Inter, Poppins)
- **Body**: Clean, readable sans-serif
- **Accent**: Mono for tech elements

### Components
- Gradient overlays with community imagery
- Card-based layouts with hover effects
- Interactive member avatars
- Progress indicators for programs
- Badge/achievement system
